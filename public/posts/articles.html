The web has tons of information about the Shroud of Turin, but not all of it is very good! As longtime Shroud followers we've put together a list of some of the more insightful resources that will help give you a good background of knowledge for attending our event. One of the best for getting up to speed about the many controversies, history, and characters involved is the Trial of the Shroud of Turin, by former federal prosecutor Lawrence E. Schauf, written in 2014.
